Our Services

Comprehensive Cleaning Solutions

We provide full-service commercial cleaning tailored specifically for your facility's unique requirements.

Office Cleaning

Comprehensive daily, weekly, or monthly office cleaning including vacuuming, dusting, and wiping down high-touch surfaces.

Floor & Carpet Care

Professional sweeping, mopping, buffing, and deep carpet cleaning to keep your floors spotless and protected.

Bathroom Sanitizing

Rigorous cleaning and disinfection of restrooms, ensuring a hygienic and odor-free environment for staff and guests.

Kitchen & Break Rooms

Detailed cleaning of employee break areas, appliances, countertops, and sinks to maintain food safety standards.

Trash & Recycling

Systematic collection and responsible disposal of waste and recyclables from all designated receptacles.

Window & Glass

Streak-free cleaning of interior windows, glass partitions, and entrance doors to maximize natural light.

Disinfection Services

Targeted electrostatic spraying and deep sanitizing to combat viruses and bacteria in the workplace.

Post-Construction

Heavy-duty cleanup after renovations or build-outs, removing dust and debris so your space is move-in ready.
